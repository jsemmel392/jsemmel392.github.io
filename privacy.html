Modern HIP — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Overview

Modern HIP is a local steganography utility for Windows. This policy describes how the application handles your data. The short version: it does not collect or move your data. Everything stays on your machine.

Data Collection

Modern HIP does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data or user information. No usage statistics, crash reports, or analytics of any kind are gathered.

Internet Access

Modern HIP does not connect to the internet. No data is sent to external servers, third parties, or cloud services.

Files and Encryption

All files and images processed by Modern HIP remain entirely on your local device. The application performs AES-256-GCM encryption and steganographic injection within system memory.
